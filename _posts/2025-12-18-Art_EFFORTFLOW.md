With the exhibition "effort + flow - mathematics and art in exchange", the Kunsthalle Barmen will be showing from 16 January to 29 March 2026 how artistic and scientific research processes can meet and inspire each other in many different ways. The opening of the new exhibition will take place on Friday 16 January at 7 pm.
